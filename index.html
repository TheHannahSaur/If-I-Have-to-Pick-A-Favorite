Chehalis River Discovery Trail

Welcome to the Chehalis River Discovery Trail in Centralia, a hidden gem for nature lovers, birdwatchers, and dog walkers.

Why It's Special

This scenic trail offers a peaceful retreat with stunning wildlife sightings, including a nesting pair of bald eagles. The trailhead is marked by a historic dilapidated barn, home to a vibrant colony of barn swallows.

As you walk along the trail, you'll find river access where osprey soar overhead, and deer can often be spotted across the water. On clear days, the tip of Mount Rainier provides a breathtaking view in the distance.

Perfect for Birdwatching

With diverse bird species thriving along the trail, it's an ideal location to bring binoculars and enjoy the sights and sounds of nature.

Visit and experience the beauty of the Chehalis River Discovery Trail for yourself!
